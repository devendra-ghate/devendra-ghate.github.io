Syllabus

Governing equations – quasi-one-dimensional flows – acoustic waves and waves of finite amplitude – normal shocks – R-H equations – shock tube problem – oblique shocks – Prandtl-Meyer expansion – wave drag – reflection and interaction of waves – conical flows – flows with friction and heat transfer – linearised potential flow and its applications – transonic flows
