Tutorial 1

Theoretical

  1. Show that for a reversible process of a perfect gas,

\[ds = c_p \frac{dv}{v} + c_v \frac{dp}{p}.\]

  1. Using above relationship, show that for an isentropic flow,

\[p v^γ = const.\]

  1. Show that for an ideal gas undergoing any process (1 to 2),

\[s_2 - s_1 = c_p \ln{\frac{T_2}{T_1}} - R \ln{\frac{p_2}{p_1}}.\]

  1. Show that for an ideal gas undergoing a process 1 to 2,

\[s_2 - s_1 = c_v \ln{\frac{T_2}{T_1}} + R \ln{\frac{v_2}{v_1}}.\]

  1. Show that the mass flow rate per unit area for a converging duct for a perfect gas is

\[\frac{\dot{m}}{A} = \frac{p_0 M \sqrt{γ/RT_0}}{\left( 1 + \frac{γ - 1}{2}M^2\right)^{\frac{γ+1}{2(γ - 1)}}}.\]

Numerical

  1. Oxygen gas is heated from 25C to 125C. Determine the increase in its internal energy and enthalpy.

  2. Air in a cylinder changes state from 101 kPa and 310 K to a pressure of 1100 kPa according to the process \(pv^{1.32} = \text{constant}\). Determine the entropy change associated with this process. Assume air to be an ideal gas with \(c_p = 1004\) J/kg-K and \(γ=1.4\).

  3. For the operation of a supersonic test-section, an air flow at the conditions \(p_1 = 6.5\) atm, \(T_1 = 440\) K and \(M_1 = 0.5\) is led through an inlet section of area \(A_1 = 160 ~\text{cm}^2\) to a Laval nozzle which expands the flow to a pressure of \(p_2 = 1\) atm at the test section. What is \(M_2\), \(A^{*}/A_2\) and \(\dot{m}\) ?

  4. Air from a high pressure task at 350 kPa and 420 K expands isentropically through a nozzle of exit area \(A_e\) = 0.22 \(m^2\). If \(u_e\) = 525 m/s, determine \(M_e\), \(p_e\), \(\dot{m}_e\) and \(A^*\).

  5. An aircraft flies at Mach 0.8 at an altitude where the pressure and temperature are 44 kPa and -15C. Determine the isentropic stagnation pressure and temperature recorded on the aircraft. Do not use gas tables.
