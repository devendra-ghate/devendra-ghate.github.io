Cholesterol and hypocholesterolemia

  • Manufactured in liver and pancreas

  • Essential for proper body function

  • Types of Cholesterol

    • HDL (GOOD) (In certain range)

    • LDL (BAD)

    • VLDL (VERY BAD)

  • Total cholesterol < 200 mg/dL (GOOD)

  • Total cholesterol (200 - 400) mg/dL (Borderline High Risk)

Major sources of cholesterol for vegiterian people

  • All milk products

  • All heated veg. oil which has trans-fat

Ratio of HDL/LDL is determined mainly because of genetics but can be modified by changing body build, medications, food choices and exercise.

People with severe chronic diseases or CANCER tend to have below-normal cholesterol levels

Hypocholesterolemia

  • Total Cholesterol < 160 mg/dL

  • Reasons

    • depression

    • cancer

    • hemorrhagic stroke

    • Aortic dissection

  • In the elderly, low cholesterol may confer a health risk that may not be offset by the beneficial effects of cholesterol lowering. (Sadly source of this information is forgotten.)
