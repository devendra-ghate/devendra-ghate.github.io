Demystifying eye examination report

  • IOP – Intra-ocular pressure Normal rang: 10 to 20 mmHg (millimetres of Mercury) The average value of intraocular pressure is 15.5 mmHg with fluctuations of about 2.75 mmHg.

  • WNL – Within normal limits

  • Sph. – Sphere Negative means near sighted. Positive means far sighted.

  • Cyl. – Cylinder

  • R – Right

  • L – Left

  • Fundus – All the parts on the back wall of the eye. That includes retina, optic disc etc.

  • IPD – Inter pupillary distance The distance between the centres of two pupils. That will determine where the bottom lenses are located in the spectacles.

  • Color of lenses – Don't know the significance of this colour.

  • PSCO – Posterior scleral canal opening (probably)
