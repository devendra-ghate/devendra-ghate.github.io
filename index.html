Design has long been considered as an art. Optimisation is usually an afterthought.

At this lab, we investigate methods and generate tools to integrate these processes tightly. Multi-disciplinary optimisation (MDO) techniques which originated in Aerospace applications and were later adopted by many industries (especially automobile engineering) are one example of this.

We apply formal optimisation techniques to problems in the area of Aerospace engineering. Past projects include airfoil optimisation, multi-modal wing optimisation, satellite scheduling algorithms and launch vehicle trajectory optimisation.
